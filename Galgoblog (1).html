What Makes the Bass, the Bass?

At first glance, the electric bass guitar looks like its six-stringed cousin, but it's fundamentally different.

Tuning and Pitch

Most electric basses have four strings, tuned to E-A-D-G (from thickest to thinnest). This lower frequency gives music its depth and warmth.

Role in the Band

The bass is the instrument of rhythm and harmony. The bassist typically works in close synchronization with the drummer, creating the groove that defines a song's feel. Without the bass, a song can sound thin and lacking in balance.
