About Me

I am Jennefer Udal, 19 years old, and currently a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) student at Davao Vision College. I am a dedicated and responsible student who strives to do my best in both academic and practical work.

I have a strong interest in technology and continuously aim to enhance my knowledge and skills in the IT field. My goal is to become a competent and professional IT specialist in the future.

My Hobby

My hobby is taking pictures. Through photography, I can express my creativity and keep memories that I can look back on. Taking pictures also helps me appreciate small details in life and the beauty of the world around me.

My Life

My friends and family are always there to support and encourage me, especially during challenging times. My sister is one of the people I trust the most, and my father teaches me patience, respect, and responsibility.
